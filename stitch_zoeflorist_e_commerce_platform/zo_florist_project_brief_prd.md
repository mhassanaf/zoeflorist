# Project Brief: Zoéflorist E-Commerce Platform

## 1. Project Overview
Zoéflorist is a modern, high-end e-commerce platform specializing in artisanal floral bouquets. The platform provides a seamless shopping experience for customers and a robust management suite for administrators.

**Brand Identity:** "Organic Elegance" — Modern, smooth, sophisticated, and user-friendly.

---

## 2. Target Audience & Roles
*   **Guest:** Casual browsers who can view the catalog, best sellers, and company information.
*   **User:** Registered customers who can manage their profile, save favorites, track order history, and checkout.
*   **Admin:** Staff responsible for managing inventory (stock/status), processing orders, and monitoring sales analytics.

---

## 3. Design System: Organic Elegance
The visual identity is built on a soft, natural palette with premium serif typography.

### 3.1 Color Palette
*   **Surface:** `#FAF4F0` (Main background)
*   **Primary:** `#5E524D` (Typography and branding)
*   **Accent (Soft):** `#F7C5B5` (Logo accents, highlights)
*   **Accent (Bold):** `#9E3B3B` (CTAs, status indicators)
*   **Neutrals:** `#D2C9C4`, `#D2C4B4`, `#BFA28C`

### 3.2 Typography
*   **Headlines:** *Playfair Display* (Serif) — Elegant, timeless, and authoritative.
*   **Body:** Clean Sans-Serif — High readability for product details and interface elements.

---

## 4. Feature Requirements

### 4.1 Customer Experience (User/Guest)
*   **Home:** Hero section with seasonal edits, curated "Best Sellers," and brand promise.
*   **Katalog Produk:** Grid-based browsing with advanced filtering by size (Kecil, Sedang, Besar), price range, and color.
*   **Favorit:** Heart-icon interaction on product cards; dedicated "Curated Collection" page for saved items.
*   **Checkout:** Two-column layout with shipping details, multiple payment methods (Credit Card, Bank Transfer, E-wallet), and persistent order summary.
*   **Riwayat Pesanan:** Chronological list of past purchases with real-time status tracking.

### 4.2 Admin Experience
*   **Dashboard:** High-level metrics (Sales, New Customers, Active Orders) and recent order activity.
*   **Manajemen Produk (CRUD):** Full inventory control including price, stock levels, and an active/inactive toggle for out-of-stock items.
*   **Manajemen Pesanan:** Transaction log with the ability to update order statuses (Pending, Processing, Completed, Cancelled).
*   **Navigation:** Seamless transition between admin tools and the public-facing store.

---

## 5. Technical Specifications
*   **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first approach for all consumer pages; desktop-optimized for complex admin workflows.
*   **Performance:** High-resolution optimized assets (HD Logo and Product Imagery).
*   **Accessibility:** High contrast for primary actions and intuitive navigation hierarchy.
